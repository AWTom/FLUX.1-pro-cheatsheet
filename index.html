FLUX.1-pro-cheatsheet

A collection of what FLUX.1 [pro] generates when prompted with "Style of [artist name]" for nearly a thousand artists. Huge thanks to fluxpro.art for generation resources, and Github user SupaGruen for the basis of this project.

Most prompts were run with 25 steps, guidance 3, and seed 1.

Updates, additions, refinement, and analysis to come.
